Tickets

Order and print your e-tickets online and there are no service or order charges.  Select your preferred seats or let our system choose them for you.  For nominal service and ordering charges you may phone toll-free 800-595-4TIX (4849).  For groups of 20 or more, please call Camille Productions at 405-603-7762 or visit Group Sales.

Tour Schedule & Venues
The first Charity Tour performance is scheduled for March 10-11, 2006 in Oklahoma City.  The Tour will then appear in different Oklahoma communities on selected dates throughout 2006. Please visit Schedule & Venues.

Ticket Prices
Charity Tour Tickets are very affordable and range from $12 to $24 for most performances.  Comparable shows in Las Vegas range from $66 to $79, and in Branson from $29 to $37.  For those wanting the best front and center seats for the Charity Tour, special Sponsor seats are complimentary with every sponsorship package.

Register for News
Tickets for each Charity Tour performance will generally go on sale about 6 to 8 weeks prior to the performance.  Check back often, or Register for News and receive notice of ticket sales, as well as other updates, special offers and promotions.  Registration is free.
