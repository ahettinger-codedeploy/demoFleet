Seating Plans » Musicals & Young Family Shows

Tent Theatre opens 1hr prior to performance for picnic dining at reserved tables and opens 30 minutes prior to performance for ticket holders. Reserved seating tickets available online, by phone from the Box office open 11AM-4PM Mon-Sat call 203-966-4634 or email boxoffice@stonc.org. Tickets can be purchased at the Tent theatre Box Office 1hour prior to performances. Theatre is wheelchair accessible. Convenient parking, easy access to theatre, performances rain or shine. In case of show cancellatin prior to first act of performance due to severe weather, tickets may be exchanged for future performances or shows.
