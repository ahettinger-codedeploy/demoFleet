Theatre





The Colonnade Theatre seats 536 and was designed by a sound engineer. The warm colors of the stone walls accented by the beautiful wood trim, elegant and comfortable seating, along with the state of the art technical equipment makes this theatre an obvious choice for performers and corporate seminars alike. The theatre features a fly loft with 17 lines, multiple stage draping, 24 input audio mixer for sound control and computerized theatrical lighting customized for each event. It also has catwalks, which make it simple to redirect any lighting, or to work on the fly lines as needed. One of the features of the theatre is the 6-foot Yamaha Grand Piano. In addition, there are men and ladies dressing rooms with makeup lighting and a bathroom with a shower for convenience. Above the dressing area there is space for set construction or rehearsals. We believe The Colonnade Theatre is one of the finest theatres of its size anywhere.
