Tickets

You can purchase tickets in any of the following ways:


By Phone
Call our Box Office at 323.957.1884 for reservations and pick up tickets at time of the show.

Online
You can get tickets online by going to either Goldstar Events or LA Stage Alliance and searching for Celebration Theatre.
You can also email us your reservation at celebrationthtr@earthlink.net with the date of the show you would like to reserve and your phone number.

In Person
30 minutes prior to the show, tickets can be purchased at the Celebration Theatre Box Office at 7051 Santa Monica Blvd. in Los Angeles. We strongly encourage you to reserve tickets by phone or online, as seats are subject to availability.

Subscribe to our 2006-2007 Season
Please click HERE for our subscription form. After you have filled it out, you can mail or fax it to us using the information on the bottom of the form.  If you have any questions about the season or subscribing, you can E-mail us at celebrationthtr@earthlink.net or call at 323.957.1884.


All tickets are considered confirmed at the time a reservation is made. We will ONLY call you back if there is an issue.
