 About DCJ:

Dianne Cole Johnson School of Dance, established in 1976 in Carrollton, has been providing the highest level of quality dance training to the Carrollton area for over two decades. Ms. Johnson feels the training of young dancers requires special attention and that it is best to commence dance training at an early age while the body is still supple and can be channeled into the proper positioning and muscle development. Every dancer's body is different and it is the teacher's responsibility not only to develop the dancer's special attributes, but also to make them aware of their limitations.  DCJ believes in developing qualities every child needs to succeed and placing them on the right track for success by emphasizing discipline, focus and self confidence.
 
This is possible only with a professionally trained staff with knowledge of the best and most current techniques in dance education. Dance instructors at Dianne Cole Johnson School of Dance are professionals with degrees in dance as well as experienced in performance and choreography.   For complete details on our professionally trained instructors, click here.

What We Offer:        

While focusing on traditional ballet technique, DCJ offers a full line of dance classes for every individual at every level.  Classical Ballet and Pointe, Tap, Jazz, Modern, Hip Hop, Lyrical, Pilates, Creative Movement, Tumbling and special classes focusing on specific aspects of dance round out the full line of curriculum.  Register on-line for any of our classes - you will be contacted with confirmation of your schedule.

DCJ is also the home of the award winning Competition Team - The Divas.  The Diva's have taken top awards at various Regional competitions, including Starbound, CoDance and Starlight.   For more information on our competition teams, visit our competition page.

For more in depth instruction, DCJ offers the Repertory Dance Theatre of Texas (RDTT).  RDTT is a non-profit performing company offering the more serious dancer age 13-adult, the opportunity to perform in more professional venues.  In addition, RDTT offers a Junior & Apprentice company for dancers ages 8-14.  Read more about RDTT.

DCJ Mission:   

To encourage and build self confidence, self esteem, artistic expression as well as instill poise and grace. We offer programs for students interested in dance at all levels, from recreational to professional with an equal emphasis on the joy and discipline of dance.
