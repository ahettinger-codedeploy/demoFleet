All Church

Angel Tree

Angel Tree is a ministry to the children of prison inmates. Often these at-risk children receive very little at Christmas and nothing at all from their imprisoned Dad or Mom during the year. CFBC's goal is to support 150 local Angels and 300 innercity Angels. You can contribute through your Adult Bible Fellowship class. Consider volunteering to contact a family, buy, wrap and deliver gifts-funds come from the donations. Each participating Sunday School Class should appoint a class coordinator. Instructions will be in each SS Class tray on October 21st, 2007. See your class Angel Tree coordinator for information and to sign up. Donations and volunteer sign-up will be October 21st through November 25th, 2007. For additional information contact Malvern Hasha @ 936-321-1390 or Stephanie Davis @ 281-440-3800.

Finding Home ... A Champions Christmas 2007

This year's Christmas production features an all new stage design, a great cast, exciting choreography, and a magnificent orchestra and choir of more than 300. This Christmas story will be told in a way you'll never forget. Act I is a Broadway-style production, and Act II is the thrilling story of Christmas complete with a huge cast, colorful costuming and the popular camels and elephant. It's a family tradition for thousands in Houston. Performances are December 8th at 3:00 p.m. and 7:30 p.m. and December 9th at 3:00 p.m. Tickets are $8.00, general admission and may be purchased beginning today in the atrium or by calling 281-586-8742.

Stay tuned for more information and online ticket purchasing.

Health Screen Specialists

Tuesday, November 6th, 2007 from 7:00am to 8:00pm [NEW]
Monday, October 22nd and Tuesday, October 23rd from 7:00am-8:00pm [SOLD OUT]

Health Screen Specialists is a mobile vascular screening company, which will have their mobile unit at the FLC on October 22 and 23, and November 6thfrom 7am - 8pm to do non-invasive, painless vascular screenings done by ultrasound. They scan the arteries in the neck, the main artery off of the heart, AND the thyroid, liver, spleen, kidneys, gallbladder, and the arteries in the legs. Average cost in a hospital is $500 each. These exams will be offered to you for $45 each or a full vascular scan for $179 (less than $23 per exam). Please call 1-800-931-4772 to pre-schedule your appointment or come by the booth in the atrium October 7 or October 14. More information...

Upward Basketball and Cheerleading

Upward Basketball is the exciting league for children in 1st-6th grade where Every Child is a Winner! Participants take part in one practice and one game each week.
