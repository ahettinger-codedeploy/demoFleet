TICKET INFORMATION

Orchestra (reserved) Seating - $20 per ticket
General Admission Seating - $15 per ticket
*$3 additional fee per ticket will apply to internet, credit card, or phone orders

Tickets Purchased at the Box Office

Premium (reserved) seating - $27 per ticket
Orchestra (reserved)seating - $22 per ticket
General Admission seating - $17 per ticket

Group tickets sales, 25 or more, are also available. Please call 731-668-7353 for details.

Where to Buy

Online:
Tickets will be available for purchase online beginning November 1. View the Civic Center seating chart, select reserved seats, and purchase tickets using your credit card.  Tickets will be mailed to you if purchased at least 2 weeks prior to the performance date.  Tickets can also be picked up at Will Call.

Via Phone:
Call our ticket hotline at 731-668-3240 to purchase tickets over the phone with your credit card.  Tickets will be mailed to you if purchased at least 2 weeks prior to the performance date.  Tickets can also be picked up at Will Call.

In Person:
You may also purchase tickets in person at Special Occasions, 188 Commerce Center Circle, Jackson, TN 38301.  Phone 731-668-3240. Regular store hours are 10 a.m. - 6 p.m., Monday - Friday; Saturday hours are 10 a.m. - 4 p.m. The store is closed on Sundays.

Tickets may also be purchased at the Carl Perkins Civic Center box office on the day of the performance beginning one hour prior to the performance.

Seating Chart

Premium seats are located in the center area of the lower level (section 3). Orchestra reserved seats are located directly to the left and right of the premium seats in the lower level (sections 2 and 4). Festival seating is to the far left and right of the lower level (sections 1 and 5) and the entire mezzanine level of the Civic Center (sections A through E). Festival tickets are not reserved and are first come, first served seating.
