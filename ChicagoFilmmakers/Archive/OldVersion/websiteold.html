To purchase tickets online, click here.

To purchase tickets by phone, call 773-293-1447 Monday-Friday, from 10am until 6pm.

To purchase tickets in person, go to Chicago Filmmakers, 5243 N. Clark Street, from 10am-6pm Monday through Friday and noon to 5pm on Saturday, October 22 and 29.

DAY OF SHOW TICKETS:
Only available for purchase at the theater box office beginning 30 minutes prior to the first screening of the day. CASH ONLY.

RUSH TICKETS:
Rush tickets for sold-out shows will be offered at the theater's box office approximately 10 minutes prior to the screening. The number of tickets available is dependent on the number of unoccupied seats. Ticketholders: You must be in the theater 10 minutes before the film's published show time or we reserve the right to sell your seat in the event of a sell-out.

PAYMENT OPTIONS:
Visa and MasterCard are accepted for phone, online and advance walk-up sales. Personal checks, money orders and cash are also accepted for advance walk-up sales. Cash only on "Day of Show" tickets at the theater box office. Note: Processing fee of $1.50 per ticket will be charged on all online and phone orders. Higher processing charges will be assessed on special admissions and passes. Returned checks are subject to a $25 fee.

REFUND AND CANCELLATION POLICY: All programs are subject to change due to unforeseen circumstances. NO REFUNDS, NO EXCHANGES. Phone and online orders cannot be changed after order is placed. Festival is not responsible for lost, stolen, or forgotten tickets or passes. In the event that a program is canceled, exchange passes will be issued only to ticket holders at the theater. DISCOUNT CARD AND PASS POLICIES: Discount Cards and Festival Passes must be presented to the theater box office on the day of the show for a ticket. Passes may not be redeemed in advance of the day of the show. Please arrive early. Admission is not guaranteed in the event of a sell-out. Even if you have a ticket, you must be in the theater 10 minutes before the film's published start time or we reserve the right to sell your seat in the event of a sell-out.
