We're proud to announce Reeling 2005 Audience Award winners:

BEST NARRATIVE FEATURE AWARD: "A Love to Hide" (France) by Christian Faure
BEST DOCUMENTARY FEATURE AWARD: "little man" (U.S.) by Nicole Conn
BEST NARRATIVE SHORT AWARD: "Mormor's Visit (U.S.) by Casper Adreas
BEST DOCUMENTARY SHORT AWARD: "Irene Williams: Queen of Lincoln Road" (U.S.) by Eric Smith
BEST EXPERIMENTAL/ANIMATION AWARD: "A Miracle" (Canada) by Daniel Barrow

Winners were selected from eligible entries. Only those films officially entered into competition in the festival were eligible. Films acquired by Reeling through film distributors were generally not entered into competition and were not eligible for awards.
