Ticket purchasing procedures are subject to change.

Ordering Tickets by Phone or Online

Tickets will be mailed to the buyer up until 10 days before the performance. If tickets are ordered less than 10 days before the performance, the tickets will be available at the venue's Will Call.

Tickets may be purchased online or by phone up until 48 hours prior to the concert. Forty-eight hours or less before the concert, tickets may be purchased at the box office for RiverPlace concerts, or at the door, prior to the concert, for PNC Bank Candlelight Concert Series and Bank of America Performing Arts Series concerts.

Will Call

A buyer needing to pick up concert tickets for RiverPlace or PNC Bank Candlelight Concert Series Shows (at Moravian's Foy Hall) should report to the Musikfest Box Office Located at Main and Lehigh Streets, across from the Main Street Depot.

PNC Bank Candlelight Concert Tickets (for Moravian's Foy Hall) may only be picked up at Will Call up until two hours before concert time. Once the box office opens (one hour before show time), tickets may be picked up in Foy Concert Hall, Main and Church streets.

Will Call hours are 4-9:30 p.m. Aug. 3 and noon-9:30 p.m. Aug. 4-13.

All Bank of America Performing Arts Series tickets should be picked up at Zoellner Arts Center, 420 E. Packer Ave., and NOT at the box office.

Other Important Information

  • When placing an order, if you do not make it through to the "Confirmation" screen after submitting your order, it DID NOT go through and you must try again.
  • Tickets are non refundable or exchangeable.
  • There is a $5 per ticket shipping, handling and service fee.
  • We accept Visa, MasterCard, Discover and American Express.
  • ArtsQuest is not responsible for lost, stolen or damaged tickets.
  • Online ticket sales will be terminated at 5 a.m. the day before the concert.
  • Your concert tickets will be mailed to you approximately three weeks from the date of purchase.

Cancellation Policy

Occasionally concerts are cancelled. "Cancelled" means the performer cancels the performance before the show commences. Should this occur, ArtsQuest will refund the amount paid for the tickets to the person who is recorded as the purchaser in our records. If a purchase was made by credit card, the refund will be made to the same credit card account within 15 days of the date of cancellation. If the purchase was made by cash or check, a check will be issued and mailed to the purchaser within 15 days of the date of cancellation. If we do not have the name of the purchaser of the ticket, the purchaser must present the tickets to ArtsQuest, 25 W. Third St., Bethlehem, PA 18015 no later than the date of the cancelled concert or 15 days from the date of cancellation, whichever is later. The Service Fee, which is applied to the costs of the ticketing system, mailing, printing and related expenses, will not be refunded, nor will there be an additional fee for processing the refund.

Lost Ticket Policy

We will replace lost tickets only to the purchaser, with government-issued photo identification AND the credit card used for the purchase, at the Box Office at Musikfest (in the case of all tickets for Musikfest) or at the Banana Factory, 25 W. Third St., Bethlehem, PA 18015 (in the case of tickets issued for events other than Musikfest). The purchaser MUST appear in person to have lost tickets replaced. There is a $5 service fee per ticket for replacement tickets.
