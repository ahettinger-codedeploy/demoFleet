The Three Goddesses Store

The Three Goddesses Store

The Three Goddesses Store is located at Wolrd Dance Center Studio at 1831-A Solano Avenue in Berkeley. The Three Goddesses stocks an exclusive variety of clothing and accessories from Brazil. Items are purchased in Brazil every season and keep current with the latest Brazilian fashion. The tropical and colorful clothing, in typical Brazilian style, is suitable for both casual and dance wear. These beautiful and unique products help to communicate the mythological heritage, sacred rites and musical roots of cultures from around the world. Such knowledge adds richness and depth to our own society while further connecting us to the ancestral traditions of others.

Artwork and crafts are handmade by skilled artisans using traditional designs and techniques. In many cases, purchases directly support small producer groups and craft cooperatives. In its latest initiative, The Three Goddesses Store is purchasing used jeans pants manufactured in Brazil, and re-selling them as a way to gather funds for BrasArte.
