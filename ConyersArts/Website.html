Credit Card Purchases : We accept Visa or MasterCard.

Refunds: There are no refunds on ticket purchases. We do allow ticket exchanges for multi-night events when possible.

Center Street Arts is an intimate experience, with only 99 seats that are provided without assigned seats. Theatre doors open 30 minutes prior to the performance. The box office opens one hour before showtime.

Please keep in mind that due to the intimate nature of the space, it is likely there will be no late seating for events.
