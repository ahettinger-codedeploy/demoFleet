Ticket Information

For information regarding concerts at Slosberg Auditorium at Brandeis University call 781-736-3400. For information regarding concerts at The Olin Auditorium at Bard College, Annandale on Hudson, New York call 845-758-7425. For information regarding concerts at The Daniel Arts Center at Simon’s Rock in Great Barrington, MA call 413-528-3595 or out of area call 800-875-7156.

Tickets to concerts at Slosberg Auditiorium at Brandeis University cannot be purchased on the Aston Magna website but are available directly from Brandeis and can be purchased by visiting go.brandeis.edu/brandeistickets, or by calling 781-736-3400 or in person at Brandeis University at Shapiro Campus Center, Atrium Box office, 415 South Street, Waltham, MA 02453.

Tickets to concerts at The Olin Auditorium at Bard College are not available on-line. Tickets to these concerts are available directly from Bard only; to order or for more information please call 845-758-7425.

Tickets to concerts at The Daniel Arts Center may be ordered online at the Aston Magna website or by calling 413-528-3595 or out of area 800-875-7156.

Children and Students:

Bring the kids for free! Up to two children, ages 6-16 may attend the Daniel Arts Center free with each paid adult ticket.

Student rush tickets, priced at $10.00, are available at the door 30 minutes before concert time on performance days at the Daniel Arts Center with a valid student I.D.

Pre-Concert Lectures

Free pre-concert lectures will be presented at the Slosberg Auditorium, the Olin Auditorium, and the Daniel Arts Center one hour prior to concert times.
