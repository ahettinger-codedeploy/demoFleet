Our Mission
Bayou City Boys Club®, Inc., (BCBC) is a Texas non-profit organization [501(c)3 status] based in Houston that was founded in 1993 to raise funds for providers of HIV/AIDS public education, patient care, and research.
  • Latest News and Press Releases

There are no paid staff members, and members are volunteers who receive no compensation whatsoever.  Over the past decade, BCBC has donated over $605,000 to Houston-area HIV/AIDS service providers.  BCBC's impact has been felt by local organizations, many of which would have been unable to meet operational expenses.

Our Awards

Over its eleven year history, BCBC has donated over $605,000 in net proceeds and received numerous awards recognizing the hard work of its members. The readers of Outsmart Magazine have selected BCBC and its events as a "favorite" in a number of categories over the past several years including:

  • Favorite Social Organization -- 2002, 2003, 2004
  • Favorite Annual Gay Event - Jungle Party - 2002
  • Favorite Philanthropic Organization -- 2003 (runner up), 2004
  • Favorite Community Organization -- 2004 (runner up)
  • Favorite Benefit of the Year -- 2003, 2004 (runner-up)
  • Favorite Fundraiser of the Year -- 2002

Bayou City Boys Club has also received an award from the readers of the Houston Voice. The award was:

  • Best Gay Non-Profit Organization -- 2004

BCBC is also the recipient of the prestigious Community Service Award from the AIDS Action Foundation.
