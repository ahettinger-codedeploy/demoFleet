Martha Brem, Artistic Director

Established in 1996 by Artistic Director Martha Brem, Artisan School of Dance Studio is based in Northwest Houston servicing Cypress, Tomball, & Spring, specializing in Classical Ballet.  We house Artisan Ballet Company, which performs full-length classical ballet programs, including The Nutcracker. Artisan School of Dance seeks to entertain, educate, and enrich the cultural growth of the Northwest Houston area through quality of dance.
