Just Believe...

An original musical written by
Philadelphia middle school students
will debut in a theater near you this spring!

Living in a Philadelphia neighborhood her whole life, Anabel has big dreams of one day attending the University of Pennsylvania. But she realizes through trials and tribulations, despite working hard and studying, homelife situations and everyday teenage problems can get in the way. When a person from out of this world visits her and shows her a glimpse of the future, Anabel opens her eyes, stays focused, and remembers sometimes you have to just believe.
