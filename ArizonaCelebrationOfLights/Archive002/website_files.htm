Come and Enjoy The Amazing Light Show!

Community of Joy presents the 9th Annual Arizona Celebration of Lights, a spectacular two-mile journey through the state's largest drive-through Christmas lights display.

Every December, the Community of Joy campus in Glendale, Arizona illuminates the desert night with a show so dazzling it sould make Santa wish he could spend his winters in the sunny Sonoran climate.

Home to Community Church of Joy, Joy Christian Schools, and Arrowhead Memorial Gardens, the 180-acre Community of Joy campus has hosted the Arizona Celebration of Lights beginning in 1997. Families now consider a drive through the lights part of their holiday tradition, and lasting memories are made as children and adults alike experience favorites such as Rudolph the Red-Nosed Reindeer, animated elves, a nativity scene, and a toy-soldier archway.

With more than 300 displays adorned with millions of twinkling lights, it is no wonder that every year the popularity of the Celebration of Lights has snowballed, as has the participation of the local community.

In addition, the Salvation Army, St. Mary's Food Bank, and Angel Tree all benefit from the thousands of visitors each year.
