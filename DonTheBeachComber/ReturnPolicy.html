Return policy- If an event is cancelled and will not occur at a later date, Don the Beachcomber will send the Buyer an email offering the Buyer the opportunity to return their tickets within 2 weeks in order to receive a refund for the full cost of the tickets, including service fees and shipping and handling charges. Don the Beachcomber, in its sole discretion, will determine when an event has been cancelled. If an event is postponed or rescheduled, the Buyer may use the tickets on the rescheduled date or, time permitting. If an event is postponed but replacement tickets are required for entry to the event on the rescheduled date, Don the Beachcomber will work to obtain replacement tickets for the Buyer.
