2007 Season Passes
Below are the SEASON PASS options for the 2007 Season.  To order Season Passes call 828-295-9627 during box office hours or click on TIX available online above.  ALL SALES ARE FINAL. 

Premiere Pass - $225
Opening night is a celebration and this is your ticket to it. We have reserved 70 of the best seats in the house on all opening nights for PREMIERE PASS holders. Not only do you get the best seats, but you are invited to all Opening Night Gatherings with the cast following the performances. Only 70 of these passes are available so order early.

Full Season Pass - $200
You won't miss a moment of great theatre. With the FULL SEASON PASS you get one ticket to all 9 productions.

Summer Pass - $145
Our expanded summer season features six great shows and the SUMMER PASS gives you one ticket to all 6

Flex 8 Pass - $195
The FLEX 8 PASS give you 8 tickets to use any way you want during the 8 show 2007 season. Use one ticket 8 times, 2 tickets twice, all 8 at once. The choice is yours. FLEX 8 gives you the ultimate flexibility

Flex 5 Pass - $135
Want the benefits of being a PASS holder but will only be in the area a short time? Then the FLEX 5 PASS may be your best choice. Just like FLEX 8 but with only 5 tickets.

Leaf Pass - $68
Our  Fall "Leaf" Season will bring three exciting shows.. Your LEAF PASS gets you one ticket to each of the 3 productions in the fall.


PASS HOLDER BENEFITS
PASS HOLDERS receive the first chance at selecting dates and seats for the 2007 Season. PASS HOLDERS also have exchange privileges**. PASS HOLDERS receive discount admission to many special events throughout the season as well as NEW VOICES OF THE SOUTH EVENTS. PASS HOLDERS receive discounts on TOPs Classes and Camps for children and grandchildren. PASS HOLDERS also receive our newsletter CENTER STAGE and receive advance notice of all upcoming events at the Blowing Rock Stage Company, plus much more

** PASS holders will have exchange privileges provided they make the exchange prior to the performance. If you miss a performance there will be a fee to move your tickets. Tickets from one production can only be exchanged for a different production if you have a FLEX 8 or FLEX 5 PASS. All unused tickets expire December 17th, 2007. There are absolutley no refunds given or credit given if you can not attend.
