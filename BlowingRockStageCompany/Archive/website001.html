TICKET INFORMATION & BOX OFFICE POLICIES

** Tickets may be purchased by calling the box office at 828-295-9627, or by visiting our Box Office location or by mail with our order form. In the summer (June through Sept 5)  the box office is located at 140 Maple Street (the old "icehouse", an old small stone building near the Blowing Rock Post Office) . During the rest of the year, The Box Office is located at our Administrative Office  located at 452 Sunset Drive Suite 2 (in the Glenwood Office Condos)

**Tickets not picked up in advance at the box office can be picked up at our booth in front of the theatre on Sunset Drive starting one hour (approx) before show time.

**Tickets must be paid for at the time they are ordered. Tickets purchased over the phone may be charged to Visa, Master Card, or Discover.

**Season Ticket subscribers have exchange privileges if seats are available and the exchange is made at least 24 hours in advance. 

** We love little children, however out of respect of our patrons children under age 4 will not be admitted to BRSC Main Stage Shows and Events.

** Individual Ticket Holders May NOT Exchange Tickets

** ALL SALES ARE FINAL - There are no refunds or credit given

** If you arrive after curtain time, you will be seated at the discretion of Management. This may mean being held outside until there is a suitable point in the show to open the door. You may be put in seats other then those that your tickets state

** Note - It is against the law for the Blowing Rock Stage Company to resell sold tickets if you have too many tickets we can not buy it back or resell it
