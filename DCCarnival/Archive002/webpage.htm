LIVE IN THE CITY - hosted by Kes (DRINKS INCLUSIVE)

Gresham, My A-List and Bakanal promotions brings you the next installment of the “LIVE IN THE CITY” brand. “LIVE IN THE CITY” 2011 will be a mega international DRINKS INCLUSIVE party with special guest and host, international recording artist Kes. “LIVE IN THE CITY” will have multiple zones with some of the best international DJs spinning pulsating soca, reggae and the best international music.

LIVE IN THE CITY will feature International Djs including Jugglers (TNT), Richie D (Jamaica), DJ Quicksilva (USA), CodeRed (Orlando), DJ Hazard (DC) and Sprang Int’l (DC), creating a vibe that will pulsate throughout the city.
