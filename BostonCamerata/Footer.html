Star of Glory

Our luminous 2012-2013 season will have, as its December centerpiece, The Brotherhood of the Star, our beloved Christmas/Epiphany production around Spanish music of the Old and New Worlds. We’ll also be exploring, in our other season programs, illumination and spirituality in North American and medieval European music, including the American première of a beautiful production from The Boston Camerata’s 2011 Reims, France residency. Your participation is requested! Stay tuned for more program and ticket information at bostoncamerata.org.
