Cleveland State is Featured on Ohio License Plate

Cleveland State University is featured on a license plate design that was approved by the Ohio Bureau of Motor Vehicles (BMV) and can be seen on Ohio’s roadways.

The special plates bear the University’s official seal and logo in green print along with the red, white and blue color scheme of the standard Ohio license plate. Of the $35 additional fee for Cleveland State’s special plate, $25 goes directly to support student scholarships at the University.

The special plates are now available at any deputy registrar agency, through the mail or on-line at OPLATES.com.
