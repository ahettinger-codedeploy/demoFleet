Partners in Education
Cobb Symphony Orchestra is proud to partner in Education with Hickory Hills Elementary (City of Marietta) and King Spring Elementary (Cobb County). Our mission to nurture tomorrow’s musicians and music lovers today is evident by our involvement in this learning community as well as by our mentoring program with the Georgia Youth Symphony Orchestra.
