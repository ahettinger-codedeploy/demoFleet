BRIGHT LIGHTS is an ongoing concert series dedicated to showcasing new and emerging composers and lyricists in musical theatre. The concerts are hosted by the incomparable Billy Bustamante several times throughout the year and fulfill one of Dreamlight’s primary goals - to nurture and support the development of new work. Past participants have included Jeff Blumenkrantz, Neil Bartram & Brian Hill, Brian Mazaferri, Matt Conner, Benj Pasek & Justin Paul, Michael Biello & Dan Martin, Georgia Stitt, Ricky Ian Gordon, Peter Foley, Alan Schmuckler & Michael Mahler, Michael Wartofsky, Sam Davis, and J. Oconer Navarro. This season we’ve taken the program to a whole new level by beginning a partnership with NYU’s Graduate Musical Theatre Writing Program; as we move forward, the final BRIGHT LIGHTS concert of each season will showcase the work of the graduates coming out of this unique program.
