DTOS Ticket Information

Good News!  Patrons will soon be able to order concert tickets online with a credit card.  Stay tuned!  We'll have the link right here and ready to go as soon as possible.  In the meantime, send us an order form and check or call today!

Good seats are available for the remaining 3 concerts of the DTOS 2006-2007 Concert Season.  Click here to access our order form in Adobe Acrobat's PDF format.  If you do not have Adobe Acrobat Reader, click here to get it for free.   Please be sure to fill out all of the information required on the form so that we can process your order correctly.  Once you have completed your order form, mail it, along with your check or money order, to Dickinson Theatre Organ Society, Post Office Box 7263, Wilmington, Delaware 19803-0263.

Pricing information for groups of 11 or more is available.  Pease call the DTOS Ticket Office at 302-995-5630 for additional information and to order tickets by phone.

To be fair to everyone, our ticket policy is as follows:

  • COD ticket price at the door is $10.00 per ticket.  Checks and cash only.
  • COD tickets are to by picked up at the DTOS Box Office on concert night between 7:00 P.M. and 7:45 P.M.  After 7:45 P.M., promised seats cannot be guaranteed.
  • Guest tickets must be arranged in advance of noon on the concert day by calling the Ticket Office phone number.
  • For tickets to be mailed, please send a stamped, self-addressed envelope.
  • No tickets will be mailed within seven days of the concert.  Tickets will be held at the door for checks received after that date.
  • All ticket sales are final.  There are no refunds or exchanges.
