Welcome to Belterra

An oasis in the Indiana heartland that offers premier gaming and entertainment opportunities around every corner. Only a short drive from Cincinnati, Louisville, Lexington, and Indianapolis, our resort features the very best in riverboat gaming, along with more relaxing amenities like a luxurious spa and world-class golf course.

From our spacious casino to our delectable array of restaurants, Belterra is the perfect place to escape to for a vacation, either for the afternoon or the week. Our hotel features guest rooms and suites that make it easy to relax and make yourself at home.

Belterra… Easy to love. Hard to leave.
