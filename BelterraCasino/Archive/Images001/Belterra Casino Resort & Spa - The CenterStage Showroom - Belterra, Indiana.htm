CenterStage Showroom

The CenterStage ShowroomSM is your ticket to musical performances, theatrical productions and big name entertainment. The state-of-the-art theatre with its advanced sound system and perfect sightlines enhances your enjoyment of big-name acts - from classic rock bands to country western stars.

The 1,800 seat CenterStage Showroom can be configured for concerts, special events and corporate meetings. It boasts a large, 25-foot screen.

The VIP room on the mezzanine level, complete with plush seats and TV monitors, overlooks the stage and can accommodate up to 50 guests
