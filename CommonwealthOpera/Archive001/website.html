For over 25 years, Commonwealth Opera has enriched the cultural life of the Pioneer Valley and beyond with quality productions of fully-staged Grand Opera, Broadway Musicals and Concerts. The company is dedicated to educating students, both young and old, about all facets of musical theater

We're very excited about our upcoming production of Rossini's Il Barbiere di Siviglia — The Barber of Seville. Tickets for the show will go on sale on October 1, 2005, and you will be able to purchase them by phone or online. Check back here on October 1st to reserve your seats!
