Who Could Ask For Anything More?

  • Nursery services for children 2 - 8 years for the nominal fee of $5.00 per child per performance for MainStage productions. Caregiver is a licensed nursery school teacher. Reservations for nursery must be made through the box office 24 hours in advance. Available for Tues. and Sun. performances.
  • Free admission for students 13 years and younger when accompanied by adults for all MainStage performances (one student per one adult), excluding Saturday night*.
  • Half-priced tickets for students 14 years through college for all MainStage and Stage II performances, excluding Saturday night*
  • Pre-show talks with artistic director Julianne Boyd every Thursday night at 7:30pm. Please join us for our very popular series and find out more about each MainStage show before you see it.
  • Post-show discussions with cast and creative staff after the first Tuesday performance of a MainStage Show.
  • Every Thursday is Community Night. Berkshire, Litchfield (CT) and Columbia County (NY) residents will receive a 20% discount on all full-price tickets. Tickets available at door, 1 hour prior to performance with ID. Cash only.
  • Senior Citizens (age 62+) 15% discount on Friday and Wednesday matinee tickets.
  • $15 MainStage Previews (first performance of each show). Best buy in the Berkshires!
  • $12 Stage II Previews (first performance of each show). A steal!

  • Handicapped accessibility with wheelchair access. Let us know if needed when you book tickets.

  • Light refreshments and gift shop.
