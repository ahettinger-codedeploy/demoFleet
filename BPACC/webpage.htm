  1. About Us

    The BPACC is a multi-purpose facility offering cultural performances spanning a diversity of repertoire and disciplines, as well as providing an ideal place for business meetings, seminars, luncheons, parties, and other gatherings.

    BPACC offers a wonderful performance season which begins in August each year and runs until June. It is comprised of professional touring artists, dinner shows, theatrical productions and a series of family shows. We consider all shows to be family friendly, but consider getting a baby sitters to have your own night out at the BPACC.

    Additionally, BPACC is an ideal place for a variety of business, social and civic gatherings.  Our conference center facilities are available for rent and are certainly considered to be a less expensive alternative to other banquet and meeting venues.  Our professional staff can help you plan your next sales meeting, training session or reception event. 

    Call us to day to find out more about our performance schedule and rental availability. 901.385.6440.
