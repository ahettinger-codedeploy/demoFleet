  • Camino Real Playhouse is your Entertainment Destination in South Orange County

    Camino Real Playhouse prides itself on being Orange County's premiere destination for live theatre. Located in the heart of San Juan Capistrano, the Playhouse is dedicated to bringing the best shows with the best talent to the stage. Actors come from as far south as San Diego and as far north as Los Angeles to work on our stage. Please make Camino Real Playhouse your Entertainment Destination!
