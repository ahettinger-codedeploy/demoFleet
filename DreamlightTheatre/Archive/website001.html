Chip Klose - Producing Director

Chip is a proud member of Actor's Equity Association, and has performed at many theatres including Walnut Street Theatre, Foothills Theatre, The Prince Music Theater, and Allen's Lane Theater. Favorite roles include John in John and Jen, Frankie in Forever Plaid (3x), and Arpad in She Loves Me. Most recently he took part in Children and Art: Sondheim's 75th Birthday Celebration at the New Amsterdam Theatre. Directing credits include Putting It Together at the Studio Theater in Philadelphia, the premiere of Three Little Birds at the Adrienne's Playground Theatre as part of Equinox, an annual festival of new plays written by young and emerging playwrights.
