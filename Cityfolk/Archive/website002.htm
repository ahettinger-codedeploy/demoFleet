ONLINE TICKETING SYSTEM

How do I access my account?
Click on ‘Account Login' on the dark bar on the left of this page. This brings you the login screen, where you are asked to enter your email address and password. Once you enter this information, you can see and modify your contact information, verify the tickets you have purchased since mid-April 2006, and print Print At Home tickets.
back to top

I've bought tickets to Cityfolk events in the past, but I don't have an account in your database. Why not?
Cityfolk elected to pre-load only 2005-2006 Celtic Series Subscribers, because this system uses a patron's email address to access all their information. We do not have an email address for all ticket buyers, so we are asking you to take a moment to enter your data, rather than create duplicate records in the system. You will only have to enter this information once.
back to top

Do you store my credit card information?
No, for your security, we do not store your credit card information.
back to top

Is my credit card information secure?
Yes. All online Cityfolk ticket purchases go through a secure server. You can verify this by looking at the "https" that starts the web address in your browser window while you're making a purchase, and look for the security icons used by your browser. Tix.com uses state-of-the-art 128 bit SSL encryption technology to insure your information is always safe and secure.

PAYMENT, FEES AND DISCOUNTS

What methods of payment do you accept?
Cityfolk accepts Visa, Mastercard, American Express and Discover cards. We also accept cash and checks -- payable to Cityfolk -- for orders placed in person and by mail.

Why are there Ticketing Fees?
Cityfolk switched to an online ticketing system, Tix.Com, for your convenience: now you can purchase tickets to all of our events, all from one source, 24/7/365. We have instituted new fees for all ticket orders to help us cover the costs associated with this system, credit card processing, box office staff, postage, and other ticketing-related costs. These fees are lower than some fees our patrons have been paying for our tickets and higher than others. We pledge to keep them as low as possible.

Service Fee: $1 per ticket

Order Processing Fee: $3 per order for mail delivery and $2 per order for all other methods of delivery. The Order Processing Fee is waived for tickets purchased at the door on the night of the event.
back to top

Can I pay for my tickets in installments?
Yes, if you place your order through the Cityfolk Box Office, you can pay in installments. Cityfolk reserves the right to require payment in full as much as one month prior to the event. If this happens, all patrons with partially paid reservations will be contacted. Tickets will not be mailed until they have been paid in full.
back to top


What discounts are available?
Cityfolk offers the following discounts. You may use one discount per ticket order.

Culture Works Passport: Buy one get one free to selected performances. To use this discount, please bring your Passport to the Cityfolk Box Office in person or fax your ticket request to (937) 223-2842.

Senior Citizens (60 and over): $2 off per ticket. Online, select the Senior price level when selecting your tickets.

Children and Students with a valid ID: Half price tickets. To use this discount, please bring your valid student ID or proof of a child 18 and under to the Cityfolk Box Office in person. On the day of an event, we offer Student Rush tickets at the door one half-hour prior to curtain, subject to availability.

I can't make it to the show. Can I exchange my ticket(s) for another show or get my money back?
Cityfolk does not allow ticket exchanges for another event and does not issue refunds. If you are unable to use your ticket, we encourage you to give it to a friend or colleague who would enjoy being introduced to something new. If you cannot find someone to use your ticket, you can donate your tickets to Cityfolk and we will issue a tax-deductible receipt equal to the price you paid for the tickets. Mail your ticket(s) to Cityfolk Box Office, 126 North Main Street, Suite 220, Dayton, OH 45402 with a short note and we will process your request. Tickets must be turned in to receive credit.

back to top

I ordered tickets, but I haven't received them yet. Where are they?
The Cityfolk Box Office fulfills ticket orders for season tickets from the first week of August to the date of the last event of the season. Ticket orders placed from August to April will be mailed within 5 business days of the date the order was placed. Room With A View tickets will be mailed in mid-June. All tickets ordered within one week of an event are held at will call.

You may want to check your account and verify whether you chose Print at Home for your order fulfillment. If so, just print your ticket and bring it with you.

If you are paying for your tickets in installments, they will not be mailed until they are paid in full.
back to top

Can I still purchase tickets from Ticket Center Stage at the Victoria Theatre?
Ticket Center Stage no longer sells tickets to Cityfolk events. Now you can purchase tickets to all Cityfolk events directly from the Cityfolk Box Office at www.cityfolk.org, 937-496-3863 or at 126 N. Main Street, Suite 220 in downtown Dayton.
