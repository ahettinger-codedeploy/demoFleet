How to Purchase Tickets

Tickets to all Cityfolk events are available from the Cityfolk Box Office.

To purchase your tickets by phone or in person, call or visit the Cityfolk Box Office in downtown Dayton. The phone number, address and office hours are shown on the bar on the left. Directions are here.

To purchase your tickets online, log in to your existing account or click on the 'Buy Tickets' button above.
