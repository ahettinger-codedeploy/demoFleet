Discover a charming Nevada hotel at the popular Carson Valley Inn. Our casino-hotel in Minden Nevada is the perfect place to rest your head.

Relax in one of our 150 hotel rooms including 10 suites and 38 deluxe rooms, all rooms remodeled since early 2011 and all with flat screen HDTVs . . . perfect for business or leisure travelers. Our 74-room Motor Lodge offers park-at-your door convenience. The adjacent Carson Valley RV Resort has 59 sites with shower rooms, pet area, full hook-ups and a laundry facility.

All Carson Valley Inn guests enjoy free WiFi, free use of a Business Center and free use of the indoor pool area which also includes two glass-enclosed hot spas and a fitness center room, both with mountain views.

The Carson Valley Inn offers 24-hour dining in Katie's Country Kitchen and exceptional values for dinner in CV Steak, a Nevada-style steakhouse. Job's Perk provides early-morning coffee plus snacks, baked good and beverages through the day.
