The Bricolage Arts Festival is a new kind of arts festival.  You won’t find it on a downtown street or even in one city, we’re not bringing in artists from other places, and the festival’s shows are only half of the project! 

Bricolage is a 4-day, Piedmont Triad-wide event presenting new interdisciplinary, collaborative works by a number of the region’s top artists.  Our 7 creative teams for 2007 include dancers, musicians, visual artists, poets, composers, actors and filmmakers, all of whom were required to create new works specifically for the festival by crossing disciplines or crossing county lines.

In this inaugural festival year, we’re proud to present these dynamic creative teams in 12 individual events being held in 11 Piedmont Triad cities (and a festival Gala at Chinqua Penn in early October!). Please check back soon for a full festival schedule and ticket information.
