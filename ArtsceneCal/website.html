The Guide to Art Galleries and Museums in Southern California
presents


ARTSCENE : 25, THE LOS ANGELES ART AWARDS
COMING WEDNESDAY, APRIL 18TH
PRESENTED BY ARTSCENE
AT LACMA (LOS ANGELES COUNTY MUSEUM OF ART)

Tickets will go on sale February 8th (sorry for the one week delay!)




Participants include:
Kim Abeles | Lita Albuquerque | Peter Clothier | Marlena Donohue | Hunter Drohojowska-Philp | Rochelle Fabb | Llyn Foulkes | Mat Gleason | Jackie Goldberg | Peter Goulds | Henry Hopkins | Suzanne Lacy | Bill Lasarow | Joni Mitchell | Richard Montoya | Adolfo Nodal | Michael Sakamoto | Ruth Weisberg

The evening will feature:
• Presentation of the Henry Hopkins Award, Rising Star Award, and Special Recognition Award
• Original performance works by Suzanne Lacy with Kim Abeles, Llyn Foulkes, and Empire of Teeth
• A panel featuring the AVR hosts, and moderated by Ruth Weisberg
• Sit down dinner catered by The Patina Restaurant Group
• Emcee: Richard Montoya of Culture Clash
• Official hosts and awards presenters: Jackie Goldberg, Peter Goulds, Joni Mitchell, and Adolfo Nodal
