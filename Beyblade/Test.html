North American Championship Ticket Information:

COMPETITORS:
Ready to become the North American Beyblade Champion?

The BBA is making it's way to Houston Texas on Sat. March 20th for the first North American Beyblade Championship.

Competitor tickets are $15.00 and go on sale starting at 10:00am (PST), February 21st, 2004 at www.beyblade.com.

There are two types of Competitor tickets:
Red Competitor tickets are to be purchased by bladers between the ages of 8 and 14. Blue Competitor tickets are to be purchased by bladers ages 15 and up!

Please Note: We anticipate this event selling out very quickly. Please purchase tickets early to avoid disappointment. We will except CASH only for tickets purchased on the day of the event. The event organizers reserve the right to terminate or cancel this event at any time, without notice, for any reason.

SPECTATORS:
Want to come and cheer on your friends and family as they battle in the BBA Tournament? No problem!

Spectators tickets are on sale the day of the event (March 20th, 2004) at Space Center Houston. All BBA spectators (with BBA competitor) will receive a special discount (50%) off regular admission prices.

BBA Spectator ticket prices:
Adults- $9.00
Senior Citizens- $8.50
Children (ages 4-11)- $7.00
Children under the age of 4- Free

SPECTATORS PLEASE DO NOT PURCHASE A COMPETITOR TICKET. ALL COMPETITOR TICKETS ARE NON-REFUNDABLE AND CANNOT BE EXCHANGED FOR A SPECTATOR TICKET.

COMPETITOR REGISTRATION:
Competitor Registration will begin promptly at 8:00am at the Space Center Houston box office.

Please print out and complete the below Competitor Waiver. This form must be signed by a parent or legal guardian and brought with you to registration on the day of the event.

To better understand the rules and regulations of the BBA Tournament, please read the Official Rules Of Play and Code of Conduct.

Don't forget to arrive early to ensure that all competitors are registered in time for the Competitor Briefing.
