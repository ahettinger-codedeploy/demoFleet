Tickets

BOX OFFICE -- The box office is located next to the theatre at 97 Hillview Avenue, Los Altos and is open Wednesday, Thursday, and Friday from 3:00 p.m. to 6:00 p.m. Will call opens 30 minutes before curtain on performance days. To purchase tickets by phone or for more information, call

(650) 941-0551

All seats are reserved, unless otherwise noted. Telephone reservations accepted with VISA or MasterCard. Single ticket sales are final: NO REFUNDS OR EXCHANGES.

GROUP DISCOUNTS are available to groups of 10 or more. Call the Box Office for details.

TICKET EXCHANGES -- Only subscribers can exchange their tickets, free of charge. All exchanges are subject to availability and we cannot guarantee the same assigned seats. Sorry, no ticket refunds.

WHEELCHAIR SEATING is available. Please notify the Box Office in advance when purchasing tickets.
