The auditorium opens one half hour before curtain time. Tickets may be picked up the day of the evening performances any time after 6:30pm or after 1:30 pm the day of Sunday matinees. We encourage those with reservations to pick up tickets at these times to avoid congestion at the box office.

Groups wishing to reserve a block of tickets may contact the box office to request information regarding group reservation policies and discounts.

NOTE: If you cannot keep a reservation, PLEASE CALL TO CANCEL OR CHANGE THE NUMBER OF SEATS so that another patron may be given the seat. Thank you!
