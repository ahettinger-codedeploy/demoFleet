Director's Special:
The Best Christmas Pageant Ever
by Barbara Robinson
December 6, 7, 8, 13, 14, 15, 2002

The Best Christmas Pageant Ever is the delightful story of what happens when
the Herdman children take over the annual Christmas Pageant. Chaos ensues
as the children (and the town) learn the true meaning of Christmas.
