Music by and book by Lynn Ahrens
Music by Stephen Flaherty

June 11-12 and 18-20, 2010

This musical murder mystery farce is based on the novel, The Man Who Broke the Bank at Monte Carlo by Michael Butterworth. Harry Witherspoon is due to inherit $6,000,000– but only if he follows his recently deceased uncle’s instructions to the letter... including taking his preserved uncle to Monte Carlo. Gun shots, slamming doors and mistaken identities keeps the audience guessing and laughing. Other works by Ahrens and Flaherty include Ragtime, Once on this Island, and Suessical.
As a proud member of Ohio Community Theater Association, CCP has been recognized on both the state and local levels for its excellence in presentation.
