February 18, 19, 25-27 and March 4-6, 2011

Set in 1946 Boston, the Tony-nominated musical comedy mystery, Curtains, features Detective Frank Cioffi. When the cast and crew of a musical become suspects after the leading lady mysteriously drops dead he is called in to solve the case. The star-struck Cioffi soon finds himself in the middle of a theatrical production and being a thespian himself can’t help but offer his own suggestions on how to improve “Robbin’ Hood! A New Musical of the Old West.”
As a proud member of Ohio Community Theater Association, CCP has been recognized on both the state and local levels for its excellence in presentation.
