The Miracle Worker
By William Gibson
October 8-9, 15-17, 2010

The Miracle Worker is the real-life story of Helen Keller, the young Alabama girl who lost her sight and hearing at the age of 19 months and whose angry and chaotic temperament is too much for her Victorian parents to handle. Luckily for them, Anne Sullivan, the 20-year-old Boston teacher is up for the challenge and eventually transforms Helen into the girl that will one day become an international symbol of overcoming adversity.
