Welcome !
Thank you for visiting the Charlestown Preservation Society website.  CPS is dedicated to preserving the architectural heritage and quality of life of Boston’s oldest neighborhood. 

Charlestown is home to the Bunker Hill Monument, Old Ironsides, Boston’s first burying ground, hundreds of Federal, Greek Revival and Victorian houses, and 15,000 people who value this rich historical heritage.

On our website you’ll find information about CPS programs, events and preservation issues.
