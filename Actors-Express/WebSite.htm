Rules of the House
  • There is NO late seating for any show at Actor's Express. The intimacy of the space does not allow for patrons to enter the theatre after the performance has begun without disrupting other audience members.
  • Your tickets will be released to an in-person waiting list 10 minutes before the performance. Please plan your travel time to arrive at least 20 minutes before the performance is scheduled to begin. For directions, click here.
  • Cell phones and any other audible personal equipment must be turned to silent mode once you enter the theatre.
  • If you enjoy the performance, please tell your friends!
