MISSION

The goal of the Independent Festival of Digital Arts (IF) is to act as a catalyst for critical and creative reflections on contemporary audio-visual culture. IF is an open forum that originated in a thriving Bay Area multimedia arts program, and now seeks to reach out to the wider community of filmmakers, digital artists, technology developers, and viewing audiences. Our festival presents digital video screenings and media art installations in conjunction with workshops, panel discussions, and presentations on media theory and practice. IF promotes exchanges that explore the dynamic interrelationship between time-based media and culture.
