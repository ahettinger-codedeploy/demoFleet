The Performing Arts Department’s spring 2010 Prism concert was a spectacular multimedia collaboration with English teacher Rob Hunter and Space and Time teacher Bill Muench. Student orchestra members and chorus members performed works by Gustav Holst, Nobuo Utematsu, Evangelos Odysseas Papathanassiou, Loreena McKennitt, Claude Debussy, and Sarah McLachlan while the audience viewed deep-field images taken by the Hubble telescope.
