"Audiences delight in this company's work on an old-fashioned main street in Glens Falls"

                    -The New York Times

ATF is the closest professional summer theater for the Saratoga, Lake George, and Albany/Capital District region of upstate New York producing new and contemporary work.  We bring the best writers, actors, and theater artists from New York City and around the country to present a season of live theater to the North Country.  ATF's mission to produce "new and contemporary" plays and musicals means you're going to see some world premieres, some of the best recent shows from Broadway and Off-Broadway, and some of the most promising "up and coming" artists working in the theater today.

Group Discounts, Dinner, and Hotel packages are available - for more information email boxoffice@ATFestival.org
