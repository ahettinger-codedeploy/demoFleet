Buy Tickets

You have 3 convenient ways to purchase tickets to ATF Shows

1. Online

Click here to purchase tickets

2. By Phone

518.874.0800

3. In Person

As a tenant of the Wood Theater, ATF utilizes Wood Tickets as its official box office.
The box office is located inside the Charles R. Wood Theater, 207 Glen Street, Downtown Glens Falls.

Hours:
Wed -Friday. 12:00 pm – 5:00 p.m.

Tix@6:
Radically discounted tickets for people 25 and under.
Tix@6 are only $10 and available for that day’s performance starting 6pm.
Tix@6 must be purchased in person, cash only, at the box office with valid ID. Limit two tickets per person.

Groups:
ATF offers discounts for groups of 10 or more. Visit our Group Discount page for more information.

Gift Certificates:
The perfect gift for any occasion. Visit our Gift Certificates page for more information.

Please note that all sales are final. No exchanges or refunds.
In the event a show is canceled, patrons will be notified by phone or email and will be provided the option of exchanging their tickets for another ATF performance or a refund. ATF accepts cash, checks, Visa and Mastercard.
